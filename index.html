Extrange creatures of the earth 🌏

By Jesús Cervantes


Our planet is full of extraordinary beings. Life is complex and wonderful, often shrouded in a mist of mystery. In this space some creatures will be shown that, from my perspective, are incredible.

The sea


An animal that feeds on the sun

Elysia chlorotica it is a type of mollusk. This being has the ability to feed on algae, but it can also photosynthesize. really? Yes, although it seems strange, this sea slug can kidnap the chloroplasts of the algae and deposit them on its back. According to some experiments, you can live up to eight months without eating algae just by photosynthesis.

Light in the sea

The sea is a strange and dangerous place. The largest creatures on earth live there, but also some of the most beautiful. Euprymna scolopes, the Hawaiian squid, coexists in symbiosis with a bioluminescent bacterium Aliivibrio fischeri. This strange combination generates one of the most beautiful phenomena in the ocean. Bioluminescence.

In mainland

Zombie land

This being doesn't exactly eat brains. It is a fungus, Ophiocordyceps unilateralis, that infects the brain of ants. It alters its behavior so that it looks for a place where it uses its jaws to fixate. Finally the fruitful body of the fungus grows and spreads its spores. In Asia, insects infected with this fungus are used as a miracle medicine.
