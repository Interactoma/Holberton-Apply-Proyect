Open forum of crazy ideas

By jesús cervantes

My dog considers that filling the floor with mud is a right.

During a long and heated discussion with my dog, where I asked and answered for both, a reflection emerged. Is it a canine right to have fun freely? Many dogs depend on humans to be able to go for a run or walk, having to wait long hours alone in the house. My dog loves to jump in puddles and run home. Why get angry? I didn't really find a reason, dogs are dogs. All dogs should be able to always have fun and be happy. Zeus, my dog, and I conclude that it is a right.
